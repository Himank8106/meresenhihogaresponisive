Our Aim

The Mozilla Firefox VIT Bhopal Is An Interactive Club Targeting Web Development. As We Know That Web Development Has A High Scope In The Upcoming Times And Our College Has Many Talented Students. Most Students Don't Get Appropriate Guidance And Opportunity To Outcast Their Skills And Advance Their Career In The Field Of Web Development. Our Club Aims To Reach Out To Them, Help And Support Them Regarding The Same. Our Club Will Help Individuals To Get Their Correct Domain So That They Can Work Precisely In Their Field And Grab The Best Placement. Our Club Will Work As The Voice Of Students In The Mozilla Community And Will Give Them Some Expert Support And Opportunity For Their Growth.
